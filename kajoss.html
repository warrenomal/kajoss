About Us

Kajoss Ltd. is a Kenyan Private Limited Company and Social Enterprise specializing in customized solutions for diverse business objectives.

Our service offerings include talent agency, event management, branding and marketing, merchandising, media production, tours & travel, content development, music, and art designed to meet the specific needs of our clients while ensuring competitive compensation for our talents.

At Kajoss Ltd., we prioritize reliable and innovative solutions that drive sustainable change. We facilitate connections between clients and professionals from diverse backgrounds, offering employment opportunities, networking platforms, and capacity-building programs.

We aim to keep business in Africa and leverage the talents of local artists, creatives, and talented youth to generate income and create a positive impact.

Kajoss Ltd.'s commitment to delivering high-quality services while making a positive social impact sets us apart in the industry. We believe in harnessing the power of business to create a better future for African youth, women, and underserved communities.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To build a sustainable and equitable Africa for future generations by promoting innovative solutions that address environmental, social, and economic challenges, driving inclusive growth and long-term impact.

Mission: We strive to create resilience and prosperity by utilizing the potential of individuals, communities, and organizations.

Key Initiatives

Creating impact with the right combinations empowers us to achieve meaningful and lasting change in our efforts, harnessing the synergy to drive positive outcomes and inspire innovation.

  • Jobs and Networking Opportunities: Kajoss Ltd. facilitates jobs and networking opportunities for talented individuals from underserved communities. Through partnerships and collaborations, we connect them with clients, industry professionals, and mentors expanding their networks and opening doors to new opportunities.
  • Personal and Professional Growth: We provide support and guidance to individuals throughout their personal and professional journeys. Through mentoring, coaching, and access to resources, we empower them to overcome obstacles, unlock their full potential, and achieve their goals.
  • Skill Development: We offer comprehensive skill development programs to youth in underserved communities. These programs focus on equipping them with in-demand skills, enhancing their employability, and fostering entrepreneurship to create sustainable livelihoods.
